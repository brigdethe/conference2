subscription projects

Ahh, subscriptions—yes, we still do them and have been since 2020. But in 2024, we prioritized quality over quantity, limiting ourselves to 5-6 subscription partners at a time. This allowed our small, nimble team to deliver work that’s timely and well-crafted. In the past, juggling too many led to stress, subpar results, and clients moving on. Lesson learned—focused work leads to happy clients.

As Snowhouse Studio approaches its fifth anniversary, I’ve been reflecting on the incredible journey we’ve had—filled with growth, challenges, and meaningful moments. 2023 was a whirlwind, a year of hard lessons and tough decisions. By the end of it, we had to scale down our team and reset our direction. It was heavy, but necessary, and it set the stage for a more intentional and focused 2024.

This past year was about finding our groove again. We embraced quality over quantity, rebuilt stronger client relationships, and celebrated steady, incremental growth. A highlight of 2024 was our collaboration with School of Motion, where we delivered a transformative website redesign that was both rewarding and fun.

I’m deeply grateful to my team, our clients, and our partners for their trust, resilience, and belief in Snowhouse. As we look to 2025, we’re focused on deliberate goals: refreshing our brand, amplifying our voice, and continuing to grow sustainably.

This Year in Review is an honest look at our journey so far—the wins, the lessons, and the vision for what’s ahead.
